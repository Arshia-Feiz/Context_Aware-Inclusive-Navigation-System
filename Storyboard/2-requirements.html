User Profiles
Wheelchair User
Needs step-free routes with smooth surfaces, ramps, and curb cuts to navigate safely across downtown Toronto.
Ramps Curb cuts Smooth surface
Student (Night Walk)
Walking home from campus late at night — needs well-lit streets and areas with low crime rates for peace of mind.
Street lighting Low crime 24h stores
Daily Commuter
Walks or bikes to work daily — wants to avoid active construction zones and road closures that disrupt the usual route.
No construction Bike lanes Fast routes
Medical Needs
Has a medical condition requiring proximity to hospitals or clinics — route must stay within a safe distance of emergency services.
Nearby hospitals Emergency access
